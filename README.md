## Overview

**Sovereign AI Workforce** is an AI research and engineering prototype developed for the **USAII Global AI Hackathon 2026**.

The project explores how a future workforce of specialized AI agents can collaborate, coordinate tasks, and support organizational decision-making while maintaining transparency, accountability, and human oversight.

Rather than focusing only on AI model capabilities, this project investigates the systems required to govern, coordinate, and responsibly integrate autonomous AI agents into real-world environments.

---

## Vision

The future of Artificial Intelligence will be shaped not only by more powerful models, but by intelligent systems that can:

- Coordinate multiple AI agents
- Manage complex workflows
- Provide transparent decision processes
- Maintain human control over critical actions
- Enable responsible AI adoption
