# Contributing to Sovereign AI Workforce

## Building the Future of Human-Governed Artificial Intelligence

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **Sovereign AI Workforce**.

Sovereign AI Workforce is an open research initiative exploring the design of
human-governed multi-agent AI systems, responsible AI orchestration, and
human-centered autonomous collaboration frameworks.

This project welcomes contributions from engineers, researchers, designers,
students, and innovators who share a commitment to developing transparent,
trustworthy, and impactful artificial intelligence systems.

---

# Contribution Principles

Every contribution should align with the core principles of this initiative:

## Human Governance First

AI systems should remain aligned with human intent, oversight, and values.

## Responsible Innovation

Contributors are encouraged to consider safety, transparency, accountability,
and ethical implications throughout the development process.

## Research-Driven Development

Technical decisions should be supported by clear reasoning, experimentation,
documentation, and continuous improvement.

## Open Collaboration

We believe progress in AI is accelerated through knowledge sharing,
constructive feedback, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
