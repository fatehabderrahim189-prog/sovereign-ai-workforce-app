# Code of Conduct

## Sovereign AI Workforce Community Standards

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating an open, respectful, and collaborative
environment for everyone participating in the Sovereign AI Workforce project.

This initiative explores human-governed artificial intelligence,
multi-agent systems, and responsible autonomous technologies. We believe that
progress in AI requires diverse perspectives, constructive collaboration, and
a shared commitment to responsible innovation.

---

# Our Principles

## Respectful Collaboration

We value thoughtful discussion, professional communication, and respect for
different experiences, backgrounds, and viewpoints.

## Responsible AI Development

Contributors should consider the broader impact of their work and prioritize:

- Human oversight
- Transparency
- Safety
- Accountability
- Ethical innovation

## Scientific Integrity

We encourage:

- Evidence-based discussions
- Reproducible research
- Honest reporting of limitations
- Constructive technical feedback

## Inclusive Innovation

Everyone interested in advancing trustworthy AI is welcome to participate,
regardless of experience level or professional background.

---

# Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on ideas rather than individuals
- Support a welcoming environment
- Respect intellectual contributions
- Follow responsible development practices

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Deliberate disruption of discussions
- Sharing harmful or malicious content
- Misrepresentation of contributions or research
- Attempts to compromise project security

---

# Maintaining a Healthy Community

Maintainers may take appropriate action to address behavior that conflicts
with these standards, including:

- Requesting changes
- Removing inappropriate content
- Restricting participation when necessary

Actions will be guided by fairness, transparency, and the long-term health of
the community.

---

# Reporting Concerns

If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it
through the repository communication channels or contact the project maintainers.

Reports should include:

- A description of the concern
- Relevant context
- Supporting information when available

---

# Our Shared Vision

Sovereign AI Workforce aims to contribute to a future where artificial
intelligence systems enhance human capability through trust, responsibility,
and meaningful collaboration.

By participating in this community, contributors help shape a more transparent
and human-centered approach to autonomous AI systems.

Thank you for helping build a respectful and innovative AI community.
